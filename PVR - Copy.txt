Sui Dhaaga- Made In India

SYNOPSIS 

Sui Dhaaga - Made in India recounts the journey of an honest man, who rises from his humble beginnings 
to become a celebrated social entrepreneur. A heartwarming story of pride, perseverance and self-reliance, 
the film talks about the need of social entrepreneurship as a tool for the social and economic 
development of our artisans, who are living their life on the margins due to the changing times.
